About Me

Jiyeon Park is an Assistant Professor of Special Education at the Department of Teaching, Learning, and Educational Leadership at Binghamton University, SUNY. Prior to joining Binghamton, she worked for nearly 20 years as an elementary school teacher, special education teacher, field supervisor, and college instructor.
